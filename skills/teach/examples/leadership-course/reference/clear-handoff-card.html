The Clear Handoff

Say these five before delegating anything that matters. If you feel the urge to hover, you skipped one — go back and fill it in.

OutcomeWhat finished thing do I want? State the result, not the activity.
Why it mattersWhat context lets them make calls I didn't foresee?
Boundaries & rightsWhat's fixed? What can they decide without asking me?
Definition of doneHow will we both know it's finished and good?
Check-in cadenceWhen do we sync next — agreed now, so I don't drop by anxiously?
Litmus test: after the handoff, could the person take one smart action tomorrow without messaging you? If not, your outcome or decision rights are still too vague.
